John Marshall Class of 2008 | 15 Year Reunion

Aug 12th 8pm-12am, LC's Venue 316 Broadway Ave S, Rochester, MN 55904

About this event

We are thrilled that you came across this page and hope to see you in person on Saturday, August 12th, 2023 at LC’s Venue in Rochester, MN!

The plan

We want this reunion to be fun and relaxing so we’ll have light appetizers, a cash bar, music, and other activities. The space is rented from 8pm until midnight so come and go as you are able. Our goal is to bring our classmates together in a no-pressure situation so dress for a night out but we care more about your company than your clothes.

We’d love to ensure that you have something that interests you at the reunion to help you have more meaningful connections with your classmates so drop us a line at rockets2008reunion @ gmail.com and we’ll see how we can include that activity with consideration of our small budget.

Lastly, we would be honored if you spread the word about this reunion to the friends you are still connected to.

Logistics

LC’s has a venue limit of 150 at any one point in time so each 2008 Rocket alum can bring 1 guest (high-five if your guest is also a 2008 Rocket alum), please leave your mini-Rockets with someone you trust while you come hang out with us!

Tickets for this event are $15 per attendee and that covers the cost of the venue, administration fees (the credit card processing), activities, and light appetizers. Any proceeds (tickets sales above operating costs) will be donated to charity. In order to simplify administrative overhead, there will be no refunds for this event.

We suggest that you grab a bite to eat before the reunion as the appetizers are light.
